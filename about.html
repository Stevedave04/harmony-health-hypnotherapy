Clinical Hypnotherapist · Registered Nurse

Hi, I'm Belle — a former NHS Nurse turned Clinical Hypnotherapist with a passion for healing hearts and minds. My journey began on the COVID-19 frontlines, where I witnessed incredible resilience alongside profound mental health struggles. This experience shaped my deep understanding of anxiety, stress, and depression.

Later, working as a Neonatal Nurse, I supported parents through their most vulnerable moments in the NICU. I am the founder of NICUandYou.co.uk, author of One Day at a Time, and host of the NICU & You Podcast.

On my own discovery of hypnotherapy, everything changed — and now my training enables me to offer others that same life-changing experience. When you work with me, you will find a space of complete safety and acceptance.
